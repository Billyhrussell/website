About Me

I graduated from San Jose State University May 2021 in Sociology with an emphasis in Human Interaction. Throughout my studies, my favorite part was learning quantitative analysis, gathering and analyzing data in order to form conclusions or get an idea of why things would happen. After graduating I started a job in QA data analysis and through working with certain softwares, I realized I wanted more, to do more and to be able to make softwares better, easier, simpler and more efficient.

After spending a lot of time thinking about my future, I believe that working on problems and creating solutions is something that I want to do as a career. I had taken a C++ class in undergrad and I remember really enjoying the troubleshooting and satisfaction once the code worked. This is what led me to make a career change into software engineering. I am currently enrolled at Rithm School as part of their 2022 cohort, starting in May and will continue to update this website with my future projects.
